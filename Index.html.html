The Challenge

Your business is growing.
Your legal and compliance infrastructure is not keeping pace.

📋

No AML Program

Under UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 10 of 2025, every DNFBP — real estate brokers, accountants, corporate service providers — must have a functioning AML compliance program. The Ministry of Economy imposed AED 42M in fines in H1 2025 alone.

Weak Contracts

Commercial agreements without governing law clauses, dispute resolution mechanisms, or IP ownership provisions. Legal gaps that only become visible when a dispute has already started.
